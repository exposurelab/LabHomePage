Instrumentation

ion chromatography (IC)




IC is used to analyze ionic species of aerosol such as sulfate, nitrate, ammonium, sodium, chloride, etc.

gas chromatography (GC)




GC is connected to two different detectors: electron capture detector (ECD)and flame ionization detector (FID) to analyze polychlorinated biphenyls (PCBs), polycyclic aromatic hydrocarbons (PAHs) and chlorinated pesticides

GC mass spectrometry




Besides analyzing PAHs, PCBs, and chlorinated pesticides, GC/MS is used to analyze source tracers such as those emitted from vehicle and fragrances.

liquid chromatography -- triple-quadrupole mass spectrometry (LC/MS/MS)




LC/MS/MS is used to analyze soluble organics in the aerosols such as tracers from biomass burning and tire dusts and organic acids; it can also be used for analyze pharmaceuticals and active ingredients of personal care products in the water environments which may be harmful to biota in the waters.
